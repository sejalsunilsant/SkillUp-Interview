Welcome to SkillUp. Here's how to master your interview skills.

1Configure Interview

Start by pasting the job description you're targetting and uploading your resume. Our AI will analyze both to tailor questions specifically to your background and the role's requirements.

2Interview Session

During the interview, our AI monitor tracks your facial expressions and tone. Speak naturally as you respond to the AI-generated questions. The system transcribes your speech in real-time.

3Analyze & Improve

After each session, get detailed feedback and a score based on your performance. Track your growth over time on the progress dashboard to see your improvement in technical and soft skills.
